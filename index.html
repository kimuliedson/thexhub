About us

A truly unique bar and dining experience located in Najeera. Offering a wide range of dishes as well as bar and outdoor facilities. X Hub features a unique ambience with tasteful décor, specially designed for your comfort. With our delicious cuisine carefully grilled; the tantalizing taste of some of Kampala’s most exciting craft cocktails, and the sounds of Kampala’s most popular and dance-worthy music. X Hub provides you with a dining experience unlike any other in Uganda.

We offer a perfect setting for eating out or hosting an event. With a 600 seat capacity, we are also able to accommodate for private functions as well as intimate or casual dining.
